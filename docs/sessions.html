The take that was never meant to be a take.

The best songs don't start in studios. They start in kitchens, on hotel decks, in the three seconds before anyone thought to press record.

Pick up a guitar. Hum something. Play a bassline no one is listening to. Koe Sessions hears the moment an instrument begins — and starts recording from five seconds before that. Multi-track, synchronized across every Amp in the house, without anyone touching a button.

When you find it later, it’s already there. Already in time. Already mixed loose. Nothing was asked of you. Nothing was performed for anyone. It was just music, in a room, and the room remembered.

Privacy, by design.

What Koe Sessions will never do

  • Record ambient conversation. Voice detection is specifically trained to ignore speech and capture only musical performance.
  • Upload to the cloud without explicit host approval. All storage is local to the Amp units.
  • Retain beyond 30 days. Recordings automatically delete unless explicitly preserved.
  • Claim any ownership. Every recording belongs 100% to the performing artist or their management.
  • Record in bedrooms or bathrooms. Only common spaces and music rooms contain microphone-enabled Amps.
  • Work without consent. A release form is signed in advance, and any participant may disable recording at any time.

Each microphone-enabled device signals its state visually: when recording is active, a soft violet glow pulses from beneath the top face. It is always visible, never hidden.
