Koe Stone

A wireless audio object. Hand-crafted in Tokyo. Limited to 100 units for the first production run. Currently in pilot with selected artists and cultural venues.

Positioning

Koe Stone is a palm-sized aluminum speaker designed around a single philosophy: one room should be a speaker, and eight rooms should be a symphony. A single Stone plays music alone. Up to eight Stones, placed anywhere in a home, form a self-synchronizing audio mesh with latency below two milliseconds between devices. There is no app, no account, no setup. The device has zero buttons. You touch it to begin.

Price & Availability

$1,995 USD / ¥300,000 JPY per unit. Production limited to 100 units, numbered 001-100 with laser-engraved serials. First shipments planned for Summer 2026. Distribution will be limited to koe.live direct, plus selected boutique audio and music retailers. No mass-market channels.

Enabler Inc.

Enabler Inc. is a Tokyo-based design and engineering studio founded in 2023. Koe is the studio’s first hardware product. Previous work includes open-source infrastructure projects, distributed audio research, and the SOLUNA festival audio platform.
