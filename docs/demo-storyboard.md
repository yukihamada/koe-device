# Koe Seed -- 30-Second Demo Video Storyboard

**Format**: 30s hero spot (16:9, 4K)
**Concept**: Steve Jobs-style product reveal. Simple. Dramatic. Emotional.
**Tagline**: "Drop it. Music grows."

---

## The 30-Second Demo

**Setting**: Outdoor festival venue at dusk. Golden hour light. A single person walks onto an empty grass field.

## Production Notes

### Tone and References
- Apple "Silhouette" iPod campaign (2003) -- product as cultural object
- Burning Man aerial footage -- scale and wonder
- Terrence Malick nature cinematography -- golden hour reverence
- The product demo IS the music. We never explain the technology. We show its effect.

### Camera
| Shot Type | Equipment | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Macro / ECU product shots | Cinema camera + 100mm macro lens (e.g., Laowa Probe) | Shallow DOF, f/2.8 or wider. The Seed must feel precious. |
| Handheld crowd shots | Stabilized handheld or gimbal (e.g., DJI RS4) | Intimate, slightly imperfect. Human feel. |
| Aerial / drone | DJI Inspire 3 or similar cinema drone, 4K 60fps | Smooth, slow movements. No fast panning. The scale should feel awe-inspiring. |
| Slow-motion (Seed drop) | 120fps minimum (240fps preferred) | The drop is the emotional hinge of the ad. It must feel weighty and deliberate. |
| Time-lapse (crowd arriving) | Locked-off tripod, interval shooting | 2--3 hour compression into 4 seconds. Light transition from golden hour to dusk. |

### Color
Three-act color progression:
1. **0:00--0:08** -- Warm sunset tones. Amber, gold, soft orange. The product glows warm.
2. **0:08--0:22** -- Transition to cool dusk. Deep blue, purple twilight. LED glows become more visible.
3. **0:22--0:30** -- Pure black and white. No color. Maximum contrast. Maximum focus.

Post-production: unified color grade across all shots. Desaturate slightly for cinematic feel. No heavy filters.

### Music
- **Original composition required.** This is not a licensed track -- the music IS the product demo.
- 0:03 -- Single sine wave note (middle C, 261.6 Hz). Clean. Digital. Recognizable.
- 0:05--0:12 -- The note evolves. Harmonics layer. Acoustic elements blend in (strings, breath sounds). Still minimal.
- 0:12--0:18 -- Full arrangement. Electronic + orchestral hybrid. Spatial mix -- the music should feel 3D in headphones.
- 0:18--0:22 -- Emotional peak. Think Hans Zimmer's "Time" but compressed to 4 seconds.
- 0:22 -- Hard cut to silence. No fade. Abrupt. Jarring. Intentional.
- 0:27--0:30 -- The original note returns, now with depth and warmth. One becomes many.

### What We DO NOT Show
- No voiceover. No narrator. No specs.
- No text saying "featuring nRF5340" or "Bluetooth 5.3."
- No exploded product diagrams.
- No app screens.
- No price.
- No comparison to other products.
- **No visible speakers.** This is the key insight: the ad never shows a speaker because there IS no speaker in the traditional sense. The entire venue is the speaker.

## Filming Requirements

### Props
| Item | Qty | Notes |
|------|-----|-------|
| Koe Seed hero unit | 1 | Matte black, functional LED. Must look final-product quality. If no working unit, use precision 3D print + practical LED (wired from behind for CU shots). |
| Koe Seed scatter units | 100 | Non-functional mockups acceptable. Same dimensions (28mm disc). Embed coin-cell LEDs for glow shots. |
| Wristband-mounted Seeds | 10 | Silicone band + mounted mockup. For crowd close-ups. |

### Location
- Outdoor venue: flat grass field, minimum 100m x 100m
- Unobstructed horizon for drone shots
- No visible permanent structures (PA towers, light rigs) in frame
- Golden hour shoot: schedule 2 hours before sunset, shoot through blue hour

### Talent
| Role | Count | Notes |
|------|-------|-------|
| Hero talent (opens the ad) | 1 | Age 25--35, gender-neutral styling. Calm, deliberate movement. No dialogue. |
| Festival crowd extras | 50--100 | Diverse. Festival attire. Natural, candid behavior -- not "acting." |

### Crew and Equipment
- Director + DP
- 1st AC (focus puller for macro shots)
- Drone operator + spotter
- Gaffer (for product shots and LED glow enhancement)
- Steadicam / gimbal operator
- Sound recordist (ambient capture for mix)

### Schedule
- **1 day of principal photography**
- Call time: 4 hours before sunset
- Block 1 (afternoon): Macro product shots, Seed drop slow-motion (controlled lighting, reflectors)
- Block 2 (golden hour): Wide shots of scattered Seeds, hero talent walk-on
- Block 3 (sunset--blue hour): Crowd arrival time-lapse, drone aerials, crowd close-ups
- Block 4 (blue hour): Final face close-up, ambient glow shots

### Post-Production
- Color grade: 2 days
- VFX: LED glow enhancement on scatter shots (practical LEDs supplemented in comp)
- Edit: 3 days (hero cut + 3 social cuts)
- Music composition and mix: 1 week (parallel with edit)
- Sound design: spatial audio mix for headphone playback (binaural)
- Final deliverables: ProRes 4444 master + H.265 web exports
