The Honest Truth

We believe transparency builds trust. Here's what you need to know.

Where Koe Loses Koe

Latency

12ms is good, but NOT studio-grade. Shure (2.9ms) and Sennheiser (1.9ms) are significantly better for critical monitoring where every millisecond matters. 12ms Shure (2.9ms) Sennheiser (1.9ms) 1ms

Range

BLE tops out at ~30m. Sennheiser does 100m with RF. For large stages, you may need our WiFi fallback mode, which adds latency. BLE 30m Sennheiser RF 100m WiFi

Maturity

Koe is a prototype. Shure has 100 years of proven reliability. They've been battle-tested on thousands of stages worldwide. Koe Shure 100

Ecosystem

Shure and Sennheiser integrate with every mixing console on the planet. Koe is a standalone system — integration will take time. Shure/Sennheiser Koe

Certification

Koe doesn't have FCC/CE/MIC certification yet. We can't legally sell in most markets without them. This is actively in progress. Koe FCC/CE/MIC

Where Koe Wins Koe

Price

2-4x cheaper than comparable wireless systems. $179 vs $500-1,200 for Shure/Sennheiser. Pro-grade audio shouldn't require a mortgage. 2-4 $179 vs $500-1,200

Open Source

The only fully open-source professional wireless audio system. Hardware schematics, firmware, and software — all MIT licensed. MIT

Crowd Participation

No competitor does bidirectional crowd audio. Turn 4,000 audience members into a synchronized instrument. This category didn't exist before Koe. 4,000 Koe

Gesture Control

Motion-based effect control via IMU. Tilt, shake, or wave to control reverb, pitch, and filters. No competitor offers this. IMU

UWB SyncUWB

Sub-microsecond synchronization across devices. Unique in the market. Essential for multi-device orchestration.

All-in-One

Transmitter + mixer + streamer + crowd system in one ecosystem. No other brand covers all these use cases. + + + 1
