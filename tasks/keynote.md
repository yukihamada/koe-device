# Koe — 30-Second Keynote

**Purpose:** Force scope. If a feature doesn't show in these 30 seconds, cut it.

---

## Script (30s)

```
[0:00] BLACK
[0:01] FADE IN — Hand placing a dark stone on a wooden table.
       Sound: faint ocean in the distance.

[0:03] The stone pulses once with a soft warm light.

[0:05] A finger touches the top.
       Sound: music begins. Fingerpicked guitar, close-mic.

[0:08] CAMERA PULLS BACK.
       Another stone on a bookshelf across the room — it joins.
       Same song, same timing.

[0:12] ANOTHER ROOM — a third stone on a nightstand joins.
       FOURTH — kitchen counter.

[0:16] EXT. BEACH DECK — two more stones on the table.
       All in sync. No speakers visible. Music is everywhere.

[0:20] A hand picks one up and carries it toward the water.
       The music follows. The other stones keep playing.

[0:24] WIDE SHOT — the entire house, humming.
       Text (small, bottom):   1 room is a speaker.
                                8 rooms is a symphony.

[0:28] PRODUCT SHOT — single stone on dark surface.
       K O E

[0:30] END
```
